About Me

Prior to co-founding AccelerateIBD, he enjoyed the challenge to develop the international presence of Iredale Mineral Cosmetics, a leading manufacturer, marketer and distributor of branded cosmetics with multichannel distribution in the US and 29 overseas markets. Since 2011, the footprint was expanded to 50 countries and the share of international sales grew from 15% to over 30%. This included extensive work in Mexico and Brazil, across Latin America and Asia, especially Singapore and S. Korea, Pan-European pricing strategies and new brand launch in Scandinavia.

This sustained export sales growth earned the company the US President's “E” Award in 2016, the highest recognition for significant contribution to US exports. Patricio is a member of the District Export Council of Massachusetts (DEC), appointed under the U.S. Secretary of Commerce.

He studied Industrial Engineering at the Universidad de Chile and attended Business School at the Universidad Diego Portales, in his native Santiago. His Chilean professional life included founding a successful entrepreneurial venture selling fire prevention equipment, executive management of the country's largest wallcovering importer, distributing 15,000 products from Europe and Canada, and managing the operations and extensive supply chain of a leading wholesale and retail construction materials and equipment group.

Naturalized in the US, he has also directed the sales and operations of a home furnishings manufacturer and then ran his own financial trading enterprise for 5 years, developing proprietary data base management tools.

A lifetime motorcyclist and trained wilderness guide in Patagonia, he enjoys mountaineering and lives in Connecticut where he and his wife are avid paragliders.

What I Do

To develop long term view of international expansion, prioritize viable opportunities, find and support partners who respect the value of collaboration in representing your brand.

To guide the strategic consideration of why and how to best develop your international presence and the implementation of sustainable operating plans.

To generate enhanced entrprise value in a way that makes your international presence a compelling, integral part of your business.

How I Do It

Looking at the brand, service and product lines and how they are positioned and marketed to understand how they need to be tailored for new markets.

Doing in depth market research and analysis to identify entry opportunities, distribution channels and their operation, competitor profiles, logistics, fiscal structures, the regulatory environment, barriers to entry, marketing standards, tax and pricing models (down to retail), legal requierements and options, and consumer profiles.

Extensively vetting and selecting potential partners, relationship building and discovery process; being unafraid to move away from the easy or comfortable choice...or to wait for the optimal fit. Renegotiating a contract or restructuring an existing partner.

Making in person market visits to understand the environment, challenge assumptions, seek inspiration, asses risk and cultural differences/realities, commence in market networking and support development, verify understanding and meet industry players, and potnetial partners. Collaboration with trade associations, chambers of commerce, officials, industry and general contacts of the US and host country.

Guiding the transition to culturally adapted marketing programs and the development of an effective commercial approach.

Diligently selecting and using trade shows as part of a pro-active market penetration plan and timeline.

Getting compliance right and considering its broader impact and implications.

Planning and executing launch events.

Establishing comprehensive but practical reporting systems.

Treating the local partner/management as an extension of the Head Office team while building the relationship.
